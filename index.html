SAGE stands for Shastric, Academic, Governing and Experiential learner.

Shastric

We teach time tested principles from our Shastras like Vedas, Bhagavad-Gita, Ramayana, Mahabharata, Bhagavatam and other Puranas.

Academic

We teach and create world class environment for academic excellence, following CBSE board and deriving principles from IB education.

Governing

We create tomorrow's leaders who have the power to govern our country or any organization with positive influence.

Experiential

We engage students in direct experience and focused reflection to increase knowledge, develop skills and clarify values.
